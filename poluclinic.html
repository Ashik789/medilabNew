Laboratory & Polyclinic

MedilabS diagnostic laboratory & Polyclinic Cheruvathur is a leading health care sector in Cheruvathur, Kerala.

We are operating with a fully automated analyzer which reduces manual error and gives perfect and fast results. We are capable of doing Biochemistry, immunology, serology, and microbiology related testes. We have a good squad for the Pathology department. We are so much confident about the detection of diseases through Pathology lab tests. Here we are using international standard quality measures that will give customers a perfect result. We have a wide range of products/services that will be useful to the customers and will avoid them to go far hospitals. Here each and every employee are dedicated to their roles and put in a lot of effort to achieve the common vision and larger goals of the company. In the near future, this business aims to expand its line of products and services and cater to a larger client base.

Our Polyclinic occupies a prominent location in Cheruvathur. It is an effortless task in commuting to this establishment as there are various modes of transport readily available. It is at Padanna road opposite to the fish market, which makes it easy for first time visitors to locating this establishment.

It is known to provide top services in the following categories: All kinds of lab tests that we detailed above, speciality and super speciality doctors, 12 channel digital ECG, advanced digital X-ray. Customers can easily park their vehicles in our large parking area.

The staff at this establishment are courteous and prompt at providing any assistance. They readily answer any queries or questions that you may have.
